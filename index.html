Explore Nursing

If you're interested in becoming a nurse, you'll need to follow a specific educational path. Here's how:

  1. Complete high school with a strong focus on science and biology courses.
  2. Enroll in a nursing program at a college or university to earn a nursing degree.
  3. Pass the licensing exam to become a registered nurse (RN).

After high school, nurses have various career opportunities in hospitals, clinics, and other healthcare settings. The average salary for nurses varies depending on location and experience.
