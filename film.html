What is it?

Film and video production is the process of making a film, from the initial idea and storyline, to screenwriting, shooting film, sound recording, and post-production editing. Films today appear not only in theaters, but broadcast TV, cable, streaming services, and multiple internet platforms like You'Tube. While it's easier than ever today to make a film with available technology, there are techniques and skills that are still required to achieve high quality production.

What will I do?

Film directors are in charge of making, leading, and developing a film production, including funding, screenwriting, casting, film locations, filming, editing, and distribution. Camera operators are responsible for managing camera equipment and filming the scenes. Film editors organize digital footage with software to create the final film production.
