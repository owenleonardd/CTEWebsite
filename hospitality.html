What is it?

Culinary arts is the preparation, cooking, and presentation of food and meals. You will usually work in restaurants, most likely as a cook or chef in the kitchen, but also in "front of the house" roles as a server, greeter, or maitre d. Catering for private events is a big business that involves preparing food for large events like weddings, conventions, or corporate meetings.

What will I do?

As a cook or chef you will plan menus, prepare food to be used for meals, cook and bake meals, and prepare the final presentation for the customer. Servers and hosts greet customers, take their orders, serve their food, and deliver great customer service. Nutritionists develop meal plans and menus that focus on improving diet and nutrition for their customers.
