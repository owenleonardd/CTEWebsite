What will I do?

Graphic designers typically work for an entertainment, video game, or advertising agency, or "in-house" at a traditional business, or even free-lance working on their own. Animators create visuals for computer programs or games. If you work in a marketing department you might design artwork and campaigns for presentations or marketing materials. Graphic designers will be fluent in computer skills such as Adobe or HTML to help create online content and websites.
