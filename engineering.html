What is it?

Engineering design is the process of creating a plan that will be used to build or make something, whether a small electronic device, a vacuum cleaner, or a machine that makes parts. The engineering design process involves multiple teams that work in tandem with one another, iterating and testing the process over- and-over, delivering a final process, solution, or prototype to be built or executed based on what's needed.

What will I do?

Engineers, engineering technicians, drafters, designers, and others work together through an iterative process that usually includes: defining the problem or what needs to be built; identifying possible constraints; brainstorming different possible solutions; prototyping the most promising solution; testing and evaluating; iterating to make improvements; and communicating and delivering the final solution or dian.
