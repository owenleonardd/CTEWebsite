What is it?

Game design is the use of creativity and design to develop video games for entertainment or educational purposes. It involves creating compelling stories, characters, environments, rules, and challenges that drive interactions with other characters, users or objects. Game design crosses the fields of computer programming, creative writing, graphic design and animation.

What will I do?

Game designers are the creative force responsible for bringing a video game to life. They design the characters in the game, assist with the storyline and goals for the game, create the "world" or environment where the game takes place, and create goals that users must achieve to make the game more engaging-
