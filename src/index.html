Welcome to CTE Pathways!

Career Technical Education (CTE) at MVLA provides career and workforce preparation for all students. Through CTE, high school students gain valuable career and technical education so they can:

- enter the workforce with skills and competencies to be successful

- pursue advanced training in postsecondary educational institutions

- upgrade existing skills and knowledge
